Trust & Compliance

Our Certifications

Fully certified and compliant with Indian and international trade regulations — every shipment backed by verified documentation.

IEC Certificate

Import Export Code issued by DGFT, Government of India. Mandatory for all international trade operations.

GST Certificate

Goods & Services Tax registration — proof of a legitimate, tax-compliant Indian business entity.

APEDA Certificate

Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority — authorises export of agri products.

Chemixo Certificate

Chemical product compliance certification ensuring our chemical exports meet international safety standards.

Udhyam Certificate

MSME Udyam Registration confirming we are a verified Micro, Small & Medium Enterprise under GOI norms.

Spice Board

Registered with the Spices Board of India — authorising export of all categories of spices and spice products.

Certificate of Origin

Officially certifies that exported goods are wholly obtained, produced, or processed in India — required for customs clearance.

Phytosanitary Certificate

Issued by Plant Quarantine authorities, confirming plant-based products are free from pests and diseases for import.
